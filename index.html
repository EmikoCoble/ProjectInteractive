Project Overview What are you planning to do?

My plan is to map ideal locations to install small wind turbines on dams where the inverted waterfall phenomenon is commonly known to happen. The Hoover Dam water bottle experiment shows this correlation. I would like to point out ideal locations in the state of Hawaii and to make a interrelation between inverted waterfalls and the “natural” wind patterns on dams.

Questions Asked

Are inverted waterfalls a natural phenomenon?
What are the main causes for this phenomenon?
